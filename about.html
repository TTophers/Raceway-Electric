About Us

30+ years of trusted electrical experience delivering safe, clean, and reliable work.

Raceway Electric LLC has been providing high-quality electrical services for over three decades. With a strong focus on safety, precision, and long-term reliability, every project is handled with the same level of care—whether it's a small residential fix or a full commercial install.

Our experience allows us to quickly diagnose issues, prevent future problems, and deliver clean, code-compliant work that stands the test of time. We believe in doing the job right the first time.

We take pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail. Every wire, panel, and connection is installed with precision and care to ensure maximum safety and performance.

Our goal is simple: provide honest, dependable service that customers can trust. From start to finish, we communicate clearly, show up on time, and leave every job cleaner than we found it.
